Brennan McDavid

I am an assistant professor of philosophy at Chapman University and a member of the Smith Institute for Political Economy and Philosophy. I ask a lot of questions about values, organization, productivity, and the good life. I focus principally on Ancient Greece with these questions, and particularly on Plato and Aristotle.

In January 2027 I will join the Hamilton School for Classical and Civic Education at the University of Florida as an assistant professor.

You can contact me at bmcdavid at gmail dot com.
